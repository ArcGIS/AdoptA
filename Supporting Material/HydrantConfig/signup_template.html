Welcome to the Social Shoveling program, and thank you for participating!

There are 3 important guidelines

1. Shovel out the hydrant - This is critical to ensure the fire department can do their job as fast as possible.

2. Dig a path to the street - Not only do they need to be able to find the hydrant, they need to be able to access it.

3. Monitor your email - we will send periodic weather updates and a way to update the status of your adopted hydrant.
