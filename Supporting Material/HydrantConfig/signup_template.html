Welcome to Social Shoveling Program

The Social Shoveling program makes it easy for community members to volunteer to dig out a fire hydrants in case of severe snowfall.

This program allows you to claim responsibility for shoveling out fire hydrants after heavy snowfall.

In the midst of winter snowstorms, buried hydrants cause dangerous delays for fire fighters. But having City employees check and clear thousands of hydrants would be a timely, costly and burdensome process.

The Social Shoveling lets us look to community members for help. This map-based web app allows individuals, small businesses and community organizations to volunteer in shoveling out specific hydrants.
