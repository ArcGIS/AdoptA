Welcome to the Adopt a Catch Basin program, and thank you for participating!

There are 3 important guidelines

1. Remove debris from grates - The grates of the catch basins can become clogged with litter or leaves, especially in the spring and fall.

2. Ensure regular cleaning - The area under the grate should be cleaned out if debris becomes lodged in the catch basin.

3. Monitor your email - we will send periodic weather updates and a way update the status of your adopted catch basins.
